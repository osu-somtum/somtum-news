# 🎉 osu!somtum v2.0 is FINALLY HERE!

After 2 years without an update and 1 year of being closed...

**WE'RE FINALLY BACK!**

Today, I'm excited to announce that **osu!somtum** is getting a *big* update — a huge jump from where we were 2 years ago. We've been developing this since **Oct 27, 2024**.

Because our previous frontend had a lot of problems, we rebuilt our own frontend from scratch.

osu!somtum v2.0 was supposed to release a year ago, but due to a failed QA check and the developer's (blueskychan_ being the only dev) personal life issues, the project was delayed.

But finally, we're proud to announce it! Let's take a look at what's new in osu!somtum!

---

## 1. 🛡️ Anti-Cheat

We already have **50+ restricted players** and counting. A lot of people were able to bypass our PP cap limit and more. After a lot of research, we finally built an anti-cheat system to support our moderation team and community!

**How it works:** If you're not whitelisted, your newly submitted scores are checked by our anti-cheat (a background check). If something looks suspicious, your account will be automatically restricted.

Anti-cheat can make mistakes, so we still need community help. If you get restricted by mistake, please contact our staff as soon as possible through our **Discord server** or at **accounts@somtum.fun**.

As an extra layer, the anti-cheat also verifies whether your osu! client is valid. We're targeting old osu! clients that make cheat injection easy.

We're continuing to develop the anti-cheat system, so stay tuned!

---

## 2. 🏰 Guild System

A lot of players asked for this a long time ago — and we finally did it!

What the new Guild System includes:
- Leaderboard ranking
- Leader and Co-leader roles
- Guild tag
- About Us section
- Default gamemode
- Guild profile and thumbnail
- Total playtime of all guild members

Check the **Guild tab** for more details!

Players can join a guild via **invite code** (for now).

## 3. 🎵 osu!dan

Currently **mania 4K only**, based on [danreform.com](https://www.danreform.com/), added after requests from our team!

This is an experimental feature. You can request more osu!dan modes on our **Discord server**. Check out the osu!dan beatmaps in the **Beatmap tab**, and read the osu!dan rules in our **docs**!

## 5. 🏅 Custom Achievements

Got an idea for a medal? You can now **submit your own custom medal/achievement ideas** — and even build them yourself — at [somtum.fun/achievements/my-submissions](https://somtum.fun/achievements/my-submissions)!

If our team likes your idea, we'll add it to osu!somtum! Check out our **Discord server** for new medals and medal discussions.

We'll be releasing a video soon on how to make a custom medal in osu!somtum! In the meantime, we've added an **AI prompt** to help you get started — check it out on that page!

## ✨ Profile Updates

**About Me**
> We've added an "About Me" section to user profiles.
> The format is similar to osu!'s About Me section and supports BBCode.

**Ranking Graph**
> Global rank and country rank graphs are now shown on profiles.

**Achievements on Profile**
> Achievements are now displayed on user profiles.
> Scroll down to see both locked and unlocked achievements.

**Animated Banners**
> You can now upload GIFs as your profile banner, making them animated!
> -# (File size must not exceed 3MB to keep loading speeds fast.)

**Favorite Mode, Playstyles & Personal Info**
> You can now set your Favorite Mode, Playstyles, and Personal Info — like location, occupation, and interests — just like the osu! website!

**Connections**
> Link your osu! profile, Discord, Twitter, and website to your osu!somtum profile.

**Enhanced Web Design**
> We spent a lot of time improving the website's design.
> Our goal is for you to get the same immersive experience we do while developing somtum.
